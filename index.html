Our Gym

Our gym is a top of the range bodybuilding styled gym. We have a range of equipment ranging from machines such as Cybex,Life fitness and Hammer Strength. We are a very accepting community at sculpt, we welcome everyone from professional lifters to beginners. We have been home to many local professional powerlifters and bodybuilders. We are also accepting of content creators in Sculpt, a number of our members film content in the gym.
